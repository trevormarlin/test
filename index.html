About Me

Engineer. Problem Solver. Builder.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Nevada, Reno, graduating Spring 2026. My passion lies in translating complex engineering challenges into clean, functional designs that work in the real world.

From finite element analysis and geometric dimensioning to hands-on fabrication, I thrive at every stage of the engineering process. I'm a Fundamentals of Engineering Exam passer with practical experience in design, simulation, and mechanical systems.

When I'm not optimizing a CAD model in SolidWorks or running stress simulations, I enjoy breaking down machines to understand them — and putting them back together better than before.
